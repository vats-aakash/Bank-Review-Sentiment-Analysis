# Bank Review Sentiment Analysis

## Project Overview

This NLP project analyzes customer reviews from multiple banks to identify customer sentiment, complaint themes, and predict satisfaction ratings.

The objective is to help banks understand customer pain points and improve service quality.

---

## Business Problem

Central banks collect customer reviews and ratings for banking services.

This project:

✔ Analyzes customer feedback

✔ Identifies positive and negative sentiments

✔ Extracts complaint themes

✔ Predicts customer satisfaction ratings

✔ Generates actionable business insights
